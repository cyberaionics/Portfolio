# Hello, World!

Welcome to my corner of the internet. I'm **Ankush Tarafdar**, a B.Tech student in Mathematics & Computing at **IIT Dharwad** (2025–2029).

## What I Do

My interests lie at the intersection of several fascinating fields:

- **Quantum Computing** — Exploring quantum algorithms, error correction, and the mathematical formalism behind them.
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography** — Building lattice-based schemes (Ring-LWE, CRYSTALS-Kyber inspired) that resist quantum attacks.
- **Cybersecurity** — From penetration testing to digital forensics, understanding how systems break and how to make them resilient.
- **Embedded Systems** — Working with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and ESP32 to bring ideas into the physical world.

## What to Expect

This blog will cover:

1. Deep dives into quantum computing concepts
2. Walkthroughs of my projects
3. Notes from competitions like ICPC
4. Thoughts on the future of post-quantum cryptography
